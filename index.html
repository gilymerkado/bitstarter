We all use cars. From the morning when we go to work or school, on weekends, vacations and as part of our routine making errands, shopping etc. But moving around with a car comes with a price. operating a car costs money: gas, insurance, maintenance, and more. The average driving cost in the US is 59.6 cents per mile. That is $8,940 a year if you drive 15,000 miles ( the average in the US). Most of the time we use a big car to transport only one or two passengers. This is a very inefficient way of traveling. But taking more people in the car that can split the costs will save a lot of money.

RideShare is a network of drivers and passengers, that are willing to share their cars and expenses. If you are a driver, RideShare will connect you with passengers looking for a ride in the same direction as you are. The system will also connect you with passengers taking only a section of your route. By doing so, RideShare increases the efficiency and reduces your driving cost. If you are a passenger, DriveShare will find a driver to goes to your destination. If no one is going there, the system will suggest the fastest route with several drivers. This multi section car sharing system promises an extremely efficient, cheap and environmentally friendly way for traveling.
